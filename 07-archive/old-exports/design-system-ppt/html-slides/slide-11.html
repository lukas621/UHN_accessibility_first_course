Key Takeaways from Guide 01

Four ideas to hold on to -- and one downloadable job aid you can keep at your workstation.

TAKEAWAY 01

Accessibility is broader than the building.

Most barriers at UHN are attitudinal, communication-based, or systemic -- not physical. Treat all four the same way.

Linked to · Slide 04

TAKEAWAY 02

Use four areas, every time.

Awareness, Communication, Environment, Response -- a shared model across every guide and every role at UHN.

Linked to · Slide 05

TAKEAWAY 03

Pause, listen, apply, adapt, seek support.

The 5-step Decision Path makes any accessibility decision repeatable -- from intake to discharge.

Linked to · Slide 06

TAKEAWAY 04

Ask before you adjust.

The patient is the expert on their own needs. Your job is to make space for them to tell you what works.

TAKEAWAY 05

Document what worked.

A short note in the chart turns one good interaction into a pattern the next colleague can follow.
