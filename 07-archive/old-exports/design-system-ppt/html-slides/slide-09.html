Inclusive Practice Tip

One small habit that compounds across every shift, every team, and every patient interaction at UHN.

PRACTICE TIP · 01 OF 18

Ask, don't assume -- and confirm what works.

Before adjusting anything for a patient, ask them how they would prefer to be supported. Then confirm out loud that what you tried is working.

Ask the question first. "Is there anything I can do to make this easier for you today?"

Offer specific support, not vague help. "Would you like me to read the form aloud, or would a large-print copy work better?"

Confirm what worked. "Was that pace okay, or shall I slow down?" Then note it in the chart for next time.
