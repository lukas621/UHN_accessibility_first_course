# GPT Image 2 — Style System for Guide 01

## Visual Consistency Anchors

All images in Guide 01 share these anchors to create a cohesive visual series.

### Realistic Photo Style (Images 01, 02, 06, 07, 08, 09, 10)
- **Camera:** Canon EOS R5, 35mm lens, f/2.8
- **Lighting:** Warm natural light mixed with soft institutional fluorescent. Golden hour undertone. Never harsh or clinical.
- **Colour grading:** Slight warm tone, desaturated shadows, lifted blacks. Similar to modern Canadian healthcare marketing photography.
- **Setting:** Modern Canadian hospital interiors/exteriors. Clean, contemporary. Glass, light wood, white walls. NOT American-style hospitals.
- **People:** Diverse Canadian demographics (South Asian, Black Canadian, East Asian, Indigenous, white). Natural expressions. No stock photo smiles. Candid, documentary feel.
- **Wardrobe:** UHN-style navy scrubs, teal scrubs, lab coats, business casual. Lanyards with ID badges. Winter clothing for outdoor/arriving scenes.
- **Recurring motifs:** UHN-style navy/cobalt colour accents in environment, accessibility features visible (ramps, wide doors, clear signage).

### Flat Vector Infographic Style (Images 03, 04, 05, 11, 12, 13)
- **Palette:** Navy (#192858), Cobalt (#245BAA), Lilac (#C48ABD), Chartreuse (#74AE54), Red accent (#C0233B), White, Light grey (#F5F5F5)
- **Stroke:** 2-3px consistent weight, rounded caps
- **Shapes:** Rounded rectangles, circles. Friendly geometry. No sharp corners.
- **Icons:** Minimal, single-colour, consistent proportions. Gender-neutral stylized figures where needed.
- **Typography area:** Leave space for text overlays — text will be added in Storyline, not baked into images.
- **Grid:** 1280x720px canvas. 60px margins. 16:9 ratio.
- **Feel:** Modern healthcare infographic. Think WHO/PHAC visual standards, not corporate clipart.

## Dimensions
- All images: **1280x720px** (16:9, Storyline standard)
- Format: PNG
- Colour profile: sRGB
