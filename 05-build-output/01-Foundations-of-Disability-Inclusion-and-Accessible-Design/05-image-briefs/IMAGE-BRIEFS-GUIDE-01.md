## Production Notes

### File Specifications
- **Dimensions:** 1280x720px minimum (16:9 ratio)
- **Format:** PNG with transparency where appropriate; JPEG for photo-realistic scenes
- **Resolution:** 150 DPI minimum for screen; 300 DPI if print versions of job aids will use the same images
- **Colour Profile:** sRGB

### Quality Assurance Checklist
- [ ] All characters reflect diverse Canadian demographics (not US-centric)
- [ ] Disability representation is accurate and dignified
- [ ] Assistive devices are depicted correctly (correct wheelchair types, proper white cane technique, realistic AAC devices)
- [ ] UHN brand colours used consistently in infographics
- [ ] Alt text written and reviewed for each image
- [ ] No images rely on colour alone to convey meaning
- [ ] Sufficient contrast ratio (minimum 4.5:1 for text in infographics)
- [ ] Indigenous representation reviewed by Indigenous advisors (Image 02)
- [ ] All images reviewed by disability community members or accessibility SME
