## Notes on Citation Use

- Legislative citations follow the *Canadian Guide to Uniform Legal Citation* (McGill Guide) format adapted for APA 7, as is standard practice for Canadian legal references in academic and professional writing.
- URLs are provided for all sources where publicly accessible links are available. All URLs were verified as of 2026-05-23.
- Learners and course developers should verify URLs periodically, as government and organizational web pages may be reorganized.
- The Truth and Reconciliation Commission's Calls to Action (specifically Calls 18-24, which address health) are referenced in scenarios involving Indigenous health content. Course developers should consult with Indigenous Health services at UHN to ensure appropriate contextualization.
