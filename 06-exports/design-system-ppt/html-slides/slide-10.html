My Action Planning (MAP)

Before you close this guide, name three small commitments. Your MAP saves to your learner profile and travels with you across the 18 guides.

Three lines. Five minutes. A different next shift.

MAP is intentionally short and private. It is for you -- not for review by your manager. The system stores only what you choose to save.

You can return to your MAP at any time from the learner dashboard and update it after a real patient interaction.
