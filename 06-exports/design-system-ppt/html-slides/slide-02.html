SECTION 01 · WHAT YOU WILL LEARN

Learning Objectives

By the end of this guide, you will be able to describe the foundations of disability and inclusion, and apply them to everyday decisions in clinical, administrative, and operational care.

01

Define disability through multiple lenses

Distinguish between the medical, social, and human-rights models of disability, and recognize how each shapes care.

02

Identify visible and invisible barriers

Recognize the physical, sensory, attitudinal, communication, and systemic barriers a person may encounter at UHN.

03

Apply the Accessibility in Practice model

Use the four areas -- Awareness, Communication, Environment, and Response -- to guide everyday decisions.

04

Use the 5-step Decision Path

Pause and assess, listen, apply, adapt, and seek support when an accessibility need is identified.
