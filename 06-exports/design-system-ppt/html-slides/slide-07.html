SCENARIO · FOUNDATIONS 01

Meet Dahlia

Dahlia arrives 10 minutes early for an outpatient appointment. As you ask for her health card, she points to her ear, then to a small ASL card on the counter. The clinic is busy and you're behind on intake.

What is the most accessible next step?

A

Speak louder and slower while continuing the intake script.

B

Make eye contact, gesture to the seating area, and request an interpreter using the bedside iPad before continuing intake.

C

Ask the person standing next to her to help translate while you keep working.

Feedback appears here after selection. Each choice opens a brief realistic outcome -- what Dahlia experiences, what the chart records, and which step of the Decision Path it reinforces.
